We're building Dash Chat — a secure messaging app that keeps working when the internet doesn't.

## Why we're building this

When the internet stops working, Signal, WhatsApp, and Telegram all stop working. For communities that depend on secure communication during shutdowns, disasters, or in areas with unreliable infrastructure, that's not good enough.

## What's next

Dash Chat is in active development. We're currently testing with partners and community members. If you're interested in getting involved, [get in touch](/#get-involved).
