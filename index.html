What We Do

We build distributed, peer-to-peer technologies that empower communities to coordinate, communicate, and collaborate — even when networks go down or centralized platforms fail.

We build things with and for partners for whom current technological infrastructure is insufficient, unavailable, and/or dangerous.

We work with people who care about sovereignty, resilience, and keeping the web human.

We’re Building Dash Chat

Dash Chat is an offline-first, end-to-end encrypted messenger that works with internet, without internet, and bridges between the two. It’s designed for communities that need secure and reliable communication even during shutdowns or unstable connectivity.
