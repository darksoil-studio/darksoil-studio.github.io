With and without internet

When the internet stops working, Signal, WhatsApp, and Telegram all stop working. Dash Chat is built for exactly those moments.

When there's internet, Dash Chat works like the tools you already know — end-to-end encrypted messages and voice notes. When the internet goes down, it keeps going over the connections around you.

Our priorities

Usable, Reliable, and Secure... and in that order

A tool that isn't used can't provide any security at all. Dash Chat is designed to feel like the messaging apps people already know — because people don't build habits around software that frustrates them. End-to-end encryption is the baseline, not a feature.
