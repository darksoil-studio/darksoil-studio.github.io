About Us

At darksoil we build software that helps existing groups of people do more kinds of stuff together. Our aim is to support groups to fulfill the needs of people in the group together. Needs that we want to support fulfilling can be things like ensuring good food is available, helping people organize the work of making things together or facilitating sharing of goods within a group.

The groups we are looking to support can be of any type. A group may be a local village, a block of houses, a church congregation, a cooperative or a book club. By increasing the type of activities that a group engages in, we are seeking to strengthen the social ties between people and gradually increase the intimacy and trust that people feel with others that they are living their lives with. It is our belief that this is foundational support for the emergence of a more connected and regenerative human experience on the planet.

How We Work

Our work is focused around creating small and modular tools that afford groups stronger collaboration and deeper relationships. We want to make sure that digital tools are first and foremost a way for people to get their needs filled outside of the digital world. We operate under the understanding that as we move towards a richer and richer ecosystem of tools available, that can be trivially installed and used by any existing groups, the higher the benefit of any one of the individual tools become. Our goal is to utilize a network effect, not by monopolizing other peoples social graphs, but by letting real world social groups mirror themselves online and supplying them with increasingly useful software to cohere. An introductory article on this perspective can be found here.

When we create software, we are careful to build directly for contexts that are asking for the software that we are building. This way of working ensures that we create things that are wanted in the world. However, as we build the tools we take care to architect and design them in such a way that they will be useful outside of the target context that we are building for. Along with the creation of the software, we spend a lot of time relating the stories of why we are building the things we are building and making sure that we make explicit how we aim to be of service both to the larger Holochain ecosystem but also to the larger planetary whole that we are all a part of.

Buyers Club

We have a buyers club in Röstånga that makes purchases from distributors and producers and gets the products delivered to our space. There the food is sorted by those who ordered it and is stored until pickup. Once an order has been made, people pay for their part of the order with a mobile pay or bank transfer to the associations account and the person that made the order pays the invoice to the producer/distributor.

While we have an association set up that can handle this with a bank account etc. This application can also be used by a group of friends that want to do collective ordering and just have one of them pay the producer and have the food delivered to them. The friends just pay the person that paid the invoice then. The app can still manage all of the logistics and produce the needed order documents that can be emailed to the producer etc. This could be used by a lot of people without prior context and also be integrated into existing spaces like a church or whatever as a powerful organizing tool for local food webs even as a simple MVP.

Funding

At darksoil we want to create the best possible open source software to empower groups to function more joyously and harmoniously together. Our aim is to be able to use the amazing power of the Holochain framework so that people can freely create groups and use all of the powerful modular tools that we are developing as they please. We want to offer these tools to everyone who sees that they can enrich themselves and the larger systems they are a part of freely. In order for us to be able to continue to do so we seek reciprocity from the communities that benefit and from any person or organization that can see the value that we are creating.

We have set up an open collective as an efficient and transparent way to receive financial support for the work we are doing. If the way we are working towards a regenerative human presence on the planet resonates with you, please consider making a small or large, one time or recurring contribution to our collective. You can contribute generally to our work or to an individual project directly. All of our work is transparently invoiced to the collective and visible on the open collective page so you can see directly what your support is enabling.
