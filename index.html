darksoil studio

What We Do

We build distributed, peer-to-peer technologies that empower communities to coordinate, communicate, and collaborate — even when networks go down or centralized platforms fail.

We build the infrastructure we need to build the apps we want. And we share those tools with others who care about sovereignty, resilience, and keeping the web human-scaled.

Projects

Dash Chat

Internet shutdown resilient messaging.

Dash Chat is an offline-first, encrypted, peer-to-peer messaging app designed for communities facing unreliable networks, internet shutdowns, and oppressive digital environments. Built on Holochain and integrated with cutting-edge p2p technologies like IROH, Dash Chat keeps conversations flowing—even without internet access—while protecting privacy and sovereignty.

It's secure messaging that works where others can't.

Corri AI

Community matchmaker.

Corri is a peer-to-peer, local-first AI matchmaking designed to help communities and events match needs, offers, and shared interests in real time. Whether it's conferences, teams, or residential communities, Edgy connects people around ideas, resources, and opportunities—without centralized gatekeepers.

It's intelligent coordination for groups to discover and act on their potential.

p2p Shipyard

Peer-to-peer made easy.

A full-stack developer suite for building, shipping, and maintaining local-first, peer-to-peer apps across platforms. From interoperable modules and zero-config developer environments to seamless cross-platform publishing, p2p Shipyard lets you focus on your app, not the infrastructure.

Build, ship and maintain your decentralized software faster and more reliably.
