    Outcomes

    At the end of the program, you can:

  • Make simple Holochain apps
  • Work as a Holochain developer
  • Understand the basics of Holochain architecture

    • Course Pre-requiste

    Basic Rust

    In order for you to receive full value from this course, you must already have experience coding in Rust.

    While most of a holochain app can be coded in Javascript using your preferred front end frameworks, Rust is required for the core Holochain functionality and integrity.

    Program Components

    Lectures

    The lectures will break down the concepts and materials in an engaging way with live visualizations.

    Guillem will be walking you through the different elements of the course and giving clear illustrations of how Holochain works

    Exercises

    Throughout the course, there are plenty of individual and group exercises along the way.

    These exercises are built into the interactive learning materials to make sure that all of the concepts land for you as a student

    Mini-hackathon

    At the end of the course you will have time to build a very small app in order to get familiar with the actual process of building.

    This will be using scaffolding tools and we will provide you with a few different app designs to choose from to implement.

    Course Details

    Overview

    This training will run over the course of two weeks beginning on Monday the 3rd of February.

    Each day has around 2 hours of theory on zoom and then there are exercises to complete before the next session (expect around 2h for this depending on person and skill).

    The live sessions will be held at:
    8 am PST / 11 am EST / 6pm CEST

    After we have gone through all the theory, you get to practice it through implementing a small app (we provide a few different designs to choose from) in the hackathon days.

    During the two weeks after the course we will give ongoing asynchronous support for students that are building things and at the end of these two weeks we also offer a live session where we answer any questions or considerations that might have come up in building.
